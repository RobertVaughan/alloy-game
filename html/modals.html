Character Breed

×
In the Gen-Cell System we use the terms Race (the root Natural species that your character belongs to), Callings (the Awakened Preturnatural type that a Natural can become), and Breed (the Supernatural type that you can become forsaking all mortality)Characters are split between types based on their Mortality. Naturals are your regular mortal types, personified by Action Movie Heroes. Preternaturals are mortals who just have a little something extra, they are a branch of mortality and may be harder to kill but they can be killed. Supernaturals on the other hand are immortals who require specialized weapons or rituals to kill and are outside the scope of mortality.
